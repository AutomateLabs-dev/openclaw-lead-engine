# Daily Lead Handling Checklist
## OpenClaw Lead Engine Operations

---

## Morning Routine (15 minutes)

### Comment Monitoring
- [ ] Open Facebook Business Suite
- [ ] Check all posts from last 12 hours
- [ ] Find all comments containing "openclaw" (or variations)
- [ ] Reply to each: "Just sent you a message 👍"
- [ ] Immediately open Messenger
- [ ] Send DM Script 1

**Time limit:** Must complete within 30 minutes of comment

---

## Midday Check (15 minutes)

### DM Management
- [ ] Open Facebook Messenger
- [ ] Review all active conversations
- [ ] Reply to messages waiting > 2 hours
- [ ] Send classification question (if at that stage)
- [ ] Send appropriate path script (Personal/Business/Agency)
- [ ] Book business calls (if qualified)

### Lead Tracker Update
- [ ] Open CRM/spreadsheet
- [ ] Add new leads from today
- [ ] Update status of existing leads
- [ ] Flag hot leads for Dave
- [ ] Note any objections or patterns

---

## Evening Check (10 minutes)

### Final Review
- [ ] Check for new comments
- [ ] Reply if any
- [ ] Review tomorrow's scheduled post
- [ ] Note any follow-ups needed
- [ ] Log day's metrics

---

## Response Time Targets

| Stage | Target Time | Maximum |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Comment reply | 5 minutes | 30 minutes |
| First DM | Immediately | 5 minutes |
| DM follow-up | 2 hours | 24 hours |
| Business call booking | Same day | 48 hours |

## Lead Classification Quick Reference

**PERSONAL (DIY Path)**
Keywords: myself, personal, learning, side project, curious
Action: Send CourseSprout link

**BUSINESS (Service Path)**
Keywords: my business, company, team, we need, clients
Action: Qualify → Book call with Dave

**AGENCY (Partner Path)**
Keywords: my agency, for clients, resell, white-label
Action: Flag for Dave → Partner conversation

**UNCLEAR**
Action: Ask clarifying question
"Can you tell me more about what you're working on?"

---

## Common Scenarios

### They ask about pricing early
Response: "Depends on path — DIY is $47/month, business service starts at $297/month. Which direction are you thinking?"

### They say "I'm just curious"
Response: "Cool — the $47 community is perfect for exploring. No pressure, cancel anytime. Want the link?"

### They want to see more examples
Response: "The post you saw is a good example. In the community I have videos showing exactly how it works. $47 to check it out — if it's not what you expected, just cancel."

### They ghost after Message 1
Action: No follow-up. Mark as "Cold" in CRM. They'll come back if interested.

## Handoff to Dave (When)

Immediate handoff if:
- [ ] They have a real business
- [ ] They want the $297+ service
- [ ] They're interested in agency/reseller

```
Hot Lead: [Name]
FB Profile: [Link]
Category: Business/Agency
Context: [What they need]
Conversation Summary: [2-3 sentences]
```

## Weekly Review Questions

**Every Friday (15 min):**

1. How many comments → DMs? (Target: 100%)
2. How many DMs → Active convos? (Target: 40%)
3. How many Active → $47? (Target: 25%)
4. How many $47 → Business? (Target: 10%)

**If numbers are low:**
- Are posts getting engagement?
- Is DM response time fast enough?
- Are classification questions clear?
- Is pricing creating resistance?

---

## Escalation

**When to ping Dave immediately:**
- Big opportunity (established business, urgent timeline)
- Technical problem you can't solve
- Unhappy customer
- PR/risk issue

**When to wait for daily sync:**
- General questions about scripts
- Process improvements
- Content ideas
