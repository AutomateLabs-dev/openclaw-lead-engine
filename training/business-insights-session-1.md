### Key Business Insights

**1. First-to-Market Advantage**
> "First to market is a really, really important thing."
- Being early in OpenClaw ecosystem
- Building skills before APIs become private
- Establishing authority before competition

**2. Value-First Approach**
> "We're not very good marketers because we just turn around and say, 'Hey, do you guys want to have that?' And we just give it to you."
- Generous with bonuses, skills, training
- Builds trust and reciprocity
- Creates raving fans who promote

**3. Community-Driven Development**
> "Your guys' comments, your guys' engagement is really important to me. That's how we come up with the ideas."
- Workshop attendees shape product direction
- Live feedback loop
- Rapid iteration based on user needs

**4. Multiple Touchpoints**
- 7-day workshop (intensive)
- Daily onboarding calls (2 PM PST)
- Members area (self-paced)
- Discord community (peer support)
- Direct access to team (hands up in Zoom)

**5. Strategic Partnerships**
- Aligning with everyone in OpenClaw ecosystem
- JV partners promote to their lists
- Software companies (Apify mentioned as unicorn)
- Platform providers (Hostinger, InstantlyClaw, etc.)

### Pricing Psychology

**Free Workshop** → Builds trust, demonstrates value
