# OpenClaw Lead Engine — Tiered Offer Structure
## Clear Pricing + Positioning for Each Level

---

## Tier 1: Starter Access

**Price:** $47/month

**Positioning:**
"We set up your OpenClaw system for you, host it, and get you into the community."

**What's Included:**
- Hosted OpenClaw environment (VPS)
- System installed and configured
- Access to CourseSprout membership
- Starter skill pack (5-7 essential skills)
- Whitelabeled onboarding videos
- Community support (Discord/forum)
- Monthly group Q&A calls

**What's NOT Included:**
- No 1-on-1 support
- No custom skill development
- No hands-on troubleshooting
- No business consulting

**Goal:**
- Low-friction entry
- Cover infrastructure costs (~$20-30/user)
- Feed upgrade pipeline

**Ideal For:**
- DIYers who want to learn
- Side projects and personal use
- People comfortable with self-serve

## Upsell Flow

### Path A: DIY → Onboarding
**Trigger:** User signs up for $47, doesn't activate within 7 days

**Email:**
```
Hey [Name],

Saw you joined the community — wanted to check in.

Are you stuck on setup, or just haven't had time to dive in?

If you're spinning your wheels, I offer a guided onboarding session — 2 calls, I'll walk you through everything, get your first workflow running.

$297 one-time. Most people are up and running in a week instead of a month.

Interested?
```

### Path B: Onboarding → Business
**Trigger:** User asks business-level questions during onboarding

**Conversation:**
```
Sounds like you're trying to deploy this across your business, not just personal use.

The community is great for learning, but for business implementation — multiple users, custom workflows, ongoing support — that's a different tier.

I do business implementations starting at $297/month with a $997 setup.

Want to jump on a call to see if it makes sense?
```

### Path C: Business → Systems
**Trigger:** Client is happy, needs more

**Conversation:**
```
You've got the foundation running. 

Now let's talk about adding systems:
- Lead Engine (quiz + landing page + email sequence)
- Sales Engine (VSL + sales page + follow-up)
- Content Engine (ongoing content creation)

Each system is quoted based on scope, but most run $1,500-$3,000 setup plus $297/month ongoing.

Which area would move the needle most for you right now?
```

## Pricing Justification

### $47/month
- VPS hosting: ~$15-25
- CourseSprout: ~$10
- Margin: ~$12-22/user
- Purpose: Volume, activation, pipeline

### $297 one-time onboarding
- 2 hours @ $150/hr = $300
- Purpose: Filter serious users, increase activation

### $297-497/month business
- Dedicated resources: ~$50-75
- Support time: ~2-3 hrs/month @ $150 = $300-450
- Margin: $150-200+/client
- Purpose: Core revenue, sustainable business

---

## Discount Rules

**Never discount Tier 1.** It's already entry-level.

**Onboarding can be bundled:** First month + onboarding = $297 (save $47)

**Business clients can prepay:** 6 months = 10% off, 12 months = 20% off

**Agency partners:** Volume pricing starts at 10+ seats
